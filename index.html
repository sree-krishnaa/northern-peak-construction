Who We Are

Building Trust
Since 1998

Northern Peak Construction was founded on a simple belief: every client deserves a construction partner who treats their project with the same care and precision they would apply to their own home.

Over 25 years, we've grown from a small residential contractor in Toronto into one of Canada's most respected general contracting firms — with licensed architects, project managers, and master tradespeople bringing deep expertise to every phase of construction.

Hundreds of custom homes, commercial offices, and large-scale renovations across Ontario, Alberta, and British Columbia — earned through referrals, repeat clients, and five-star reviews.

How We Work

Our Construction
Process

A clear, six-step process that keeps every project on track from the first conversation to key handover.

01

Free Consultation

We meet to understand your goals, timeline, and budget. No pressure — honest conversation about what's possible.

02

Site Assessment

Our team conducts a thorough site inspection, gathering measurements, soil data, and existing conditions to inform planning.

03

Detailed Proposal

You receive a transparent, line-item proposal covering materials, labour, timeline, and payment milestones. No surprises.

04

Planning & Permits

We coordinate all municipal permits, engineering drawings, and scheduling — taking the administrative burden off your plate.

05

Construction

Skilled crews execute with precision. Weekly progress reports, photo updates, and site meetings keep you fully informed.

06

Final Walkthrough

A comprehensive inspection with you and our quality team. We don't hand over the keys until every detail meets your approval.
